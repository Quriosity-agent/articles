# Paperclip: Turning Agents from Tools into an Organizational Operating System

> **TL;DR**: `paperclipai/paperclip` is not just another coding assistant wrapper. It aims to orchestrate autonomous companies: org charts, goal alignment, budget controls, governance, heartbeats, and auditability — across heterogeneous agents (OpenClaw, Codex, Claude, Cursor).

![Paperclip Header](paperclip-header.png)

## Core Thesis
When you scale from 1 agent to 20 agents, the bottleneck shifts from model quality to organizational coordination.

Paperclip focuses on:
- role hierarchy and delegation
- goal ancestry per task
- budget enforcement per agent
- heartbeat/event-driven operation
- multi-company isolation
- full traceability

## Compared to QAgent
- **QAgent**: strong execution engine for engineering workflows
- **Paperclip**: broader organizational control plane for autonomous agent companies

## Who Should Care
Best for teams running many concurrent agents with governance needs. Overkill for solo users with 1-2 agents.
