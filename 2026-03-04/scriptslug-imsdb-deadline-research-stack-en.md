# Script Slug + IMSDb + Deadline: The Research Triangle for Writers & Directors

> **TL;DR**: If ShotDeck/Frameset/Flim solve visual references, Script Slug/IMSDb/Deadline solve script literacy and market awareness. Script Slug + IMSDb help you study story structure; Deadline tells you what the industry is buying now.

## Role Split
- **Script Slug**: modern screenplay learning/reference hub
- **IMSDb**: large classic script library by genre/alphabet
- **Deadline**: industry intelligence (deals, releases, strategy)

## Practical Workflow
1. Read 3-5 similar scripts (Script Slug/IMSDb)
2. Check 30-day market signals (Deadline)
3. Build a 30-60s proof in QCut Director Panel

## Why This Matters
Text-first + market-first planning reduces random execution. You decide what to tell, for whom, and why now — before production.
