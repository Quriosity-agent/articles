## What Is Agentic Video Editing?

Agentic Video Editing isn't just "AI adds a filter" or "auto-generate subtitles." It's a **fundamentally new paradigm** where AI agents serve as your video editing partners, capable of:

1. **Understanding video content** â€” watching and comprehending massive amounts of footage
2. **Analyzing and planning** â€” formulating editing strategies like a professional editor
3. **Using tools to execute** â€” directly operating editing software, not just suggesting changes

The key word is **"Agentic"** â€” not a passive tool, but an active agent. It can not only execute your commands ("cut this section") but proactively propose solutions ("I think the opening is too slow â€” here's how I'd restructure it...").

## Why Now? Three Technical Breakthroughs

Moore identifies three critical developments that have made this moment possible:

### 1. Vision Models Can Finally Process Large Amounts of Video

You have to understand video before you can edit it â€” and that's a non-trivial challenge. Even a short clip contains enormous amounts of information.

Recent breakthroughs in multimodal LLMs have changed the game:
- **Gemini 3** can process up to an hour of video
- **GPT-5.2**, **Molmo 2**, and **Vidi2** are inherently multimodal with longer context windows

You can upload video as input and ask models to generate timestamped labels, find specific moments, or summarize what's happening.

### 2. Models Can Now Use Tools

AI video editors need to take action â€” not just describe what's happening. We're seeing meaningful progress around LLMs as real agents that can use tools.

### 3. Image and Video Generation Models Have Reached Quality Thresholds

Moore believes many video production pipelines will be **hybrid** â€” mixing AI-generated and filmed content:
- Film documentary interviews, but generate B-roll or historical footage with AI
- Use motion transfer models to apply reference animations to real characters

These use cases require models to reach sufficient quality and consistency. That threshold has now been crossed.
